League of Legends - Top Lane

Top Lane Champions available in League of Legends

Aatrox

Aatrox smashes the ground, damaging the first enemy hit and briefly slowing them. If it is a champion or large monster, they are chained to the impact area.

Tahm Kench

Tahm Kench puts the heft of his immense body behind his attacks and abilities, gaining extra damage based on percentage of his total health.

Kayle

Kayle starts the game as a melee fighter who due to her heavy reliance on items and levels, is arguably one of the weakest champions in the early game.
